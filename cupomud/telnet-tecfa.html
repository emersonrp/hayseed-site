JAVA TECFAMoo Connection (Cup0mud)

TecfaMOO is a Virtual Space for educational technology, education, research and, life at TECFA, School of Psychology and Education, University of Geneva, Switzerland. The TecfaMOO Project page has information about the MOO and also includes manuals.

You also can look at this virtual environment from the WWW, but use a MOO client or this applet for real time communication

Short help is available in various form, e.g: beginner's help or in the TecfaMOO short guide.

Applet documentation, this Cup-O MUD Client has been written by Alex Steward.
