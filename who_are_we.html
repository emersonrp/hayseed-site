Who The Hell are we anyway?

We are a community of geek minded folk who decided to learn by doing back when this newfangled "web" thing was just being invented. The core folks are two network engineers, Rabbit and Dingo, and a sysadmin/coder, Emerson. The whole thing started out as something of a prank and has evolved into a small community of Bay Area geeks who are looking for a shell account, mayhaps an email account, and some good geek company.
