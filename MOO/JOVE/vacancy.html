Editorial Board Vacancy

The Journal of MUD Research is seeking an additional editor to serve on JOMR's editorial board. JOMR editors select outsider referees to provide reviews of submitted manuscripts, and integrate these reviews with their own impressions to recommend the disposition of articles. JOMR publishes twice a year, and currently has 3 editors.

The ideal candidate holds a graduate degree, has extensive and varied MUD experience, has excellent editorial and analytic skills, and can communicate criticism in a thoughtful, constructive, and professional manner. Experience with academic journals or books (as a pubilshed author, referee, or editor) is a plus, as is empirical research work.

Interested candidates should send a letter of interest along with a resume or vita to jomr@journal.pennmush.org by September 30, 1997.
